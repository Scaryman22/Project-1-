White Water Rafting

Embarking on a white water rafting trip through the Grand Canyon is an experience of a lifetime. As the rafting expedition begins, one is immediately struck by the breathtaking scenery of towering rock formations, vibrant blue water, and the sheer size and scope of the canyon itself. The rapids provide an exhilarating rush of adrenaline as the raft plunges through the white water and waves crash over the sides. In between rapids, there are peaceful stretches of calm water where one can relax and take in the beauty of the canyon. The trip is also an opportunity to learn about the history and geology of the Grand Canyon, as well as the unique flora and fauna that inhabit the area. Overall, a white water rafting trip through the Grand Canyon is an unforgettable adventure that combines excitement, natural beauty, and educational enrichment.

Canyoneering

Exploring the Grand Canyon through canyoneering is a thrilling and unforgettable adventure. Descending into the depths of the canyon on foot, one can experience the beauty of the landscape up close and personal. Canyoneering involves navigating through narrow passages, rappelling down steep cliffs, and wading through crystal clear pools of water. As you make your way through the twists and turns of the canyon, the geological formations and rock layers become a stunning display of natural art. Along the way, one can also encounter unique flora and fauna, such as desert bighorn sheep, rattlesnakes, and various species of cacti. Canyoneering in the Grand Canyon requires a certain level of physical fitness and mental toughness, but the rewards are well worth it. The experience is not only an adventure but also a lesson in the power and majesty of nature.

Combination

We have some longer trips that involve both canyoneering and white water rafting. These are our most sought after adventures and will leave you wanting more. Not only will you get to enjoy the adventure of ascending into the great depths of the largest canyon in the world, but once down there we will have rafts ready for your enjoyment down the beautiful and powerful water.
